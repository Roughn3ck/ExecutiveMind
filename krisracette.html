About Kris

Kris Racette is an AI architect and blockchain strategist based in Brisbane, Australia. As the founder of Executive Mind, he pioneered the use of autonomous AI agent teams for real-time business intelligence, portfolio management, and cybersecurity operations.

With deep expertise spanning artificial intelligence, decentralized finance (DeFi), and enterprise automation, Kris designed the Sentinel Protocol — an AI-powered system that monitors and manages liquidity positions across multiple blockchain networks, processing real-time market data through Binance WebSockets and on-chain analytics.

His approach combines pragmatism with innovation: building powerful systems using open-source intelligence, local AI models, and cost-optimized cloud compute. The "Dogpound" — his 20-agent AI team — operates across multiple model providers, delivering enterprise-grade capabilities at startup costs.

Kris's philosophy is straightforward: "Stay ready to thrash, keep it local when you can, and use the free intelligence to audit every paid cent spent."
